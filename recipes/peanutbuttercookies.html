Peanut Butter Cookies

These easy cookies are a delicious hit with both children and adults alike. They are gluten free and only take 12 minutes in the oven.

Ingredients

  • 200g Peanut Butter
  • 1/4 teaspoon Table salt
  • 1 large egg
  • 175g Golden caster sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180c/160c fan/Gas 4
  2. Line 2 large baking trays with baking parchment.
  3. Mix the peanut butter, salt, and sugar together with a wooden spoon.
  4. Add the egg and mix until it forms a dough.
  5. Divide into small balls and divide between the 2 baking trays.
  6. Press them down with the back of the fork (at this point the mixture can be frozen for upto 2 months. You can cook from frozen but add an extra 2 minutes)
  7. Cook for 12 minutes or until golden around the edges
  8. Cool slightly on the tray and then transfer to a wire rack.
